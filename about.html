About CampusLife Planner

Purpose of the App

CampusLife Planner is a student-focused productivity system designed to help manage academic responsibilities in one organized space. The app allows users to track assignments, schedules, classes, and events while providing helpful dashboard insights.

The goal of the system is to simplify campus life by improving time management, reducing missed deadlines, and giving students clear visibility into their workload.
